what is mental illness

Mental illness encompasses a wide range of conditions that affect a person's thinking, mood, and behavior, significantly impacting their ability to cope with daily life, relationships, and work. These illnesses can vary in severity and duration, with some individuals experiencing temporary challenges while others may face long-term, debilitating effects. Common examples include depression, anxiety disorders, and schizophrenia, each presenting with unique symptoms and requiring tailored approaches to treatment and support. Importantly, mental illness is treatable, and early intervention can significantly improve outcomes and promote recovery.
